Facetious

  • Uses

    Facetious is an adjective meaning “inappropriately humorous.”

    - It can also be used in a less negative way to mean “amusing” or, more negatively, to describe someone as “treating serious issues lightly.”

    - Examples: Facetious in a sentence Helena doesn't like Leo because he made a facetious remark at her wedding.

Synonym : joking, teasing
Antonym : serious
